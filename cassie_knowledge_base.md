## 4. SUBSIDIES & FUNDING

### Individual Funding

| Funding Type | Who Qualifies | Details |
|---|---|---|
| SSG Subsidy (MCES 21–39) | Singapore Citizens aged 21–39 | ~45% subsidy off full fees |
| SSG Subsidy (MCES 40+) | Singapore Citizens aged 40+ | ~65% subsidy off full fees |
| Singapore PR | PRs aged 21+ | ~30% subsidy off full fees |
| SkillsFuture Credit | Singapore Citizens | Offset remaining fees; claim at myskillsfuture.gov.sg using TGS code **before** course start |
| UTAP (NTUC) | NTUC members | Up to 50% co-funding on remaining fees, capped at $250/year |
| PSEA | Singapore Citizens (eligible courses) | Post-Secondary Education Account |

### Company / Corporate Funding

| Funding Type | Details |
|---|---|
| Absentee Payroll (AP) | $4.50/hr per trainee; capped at $100,000/enterprise/year |
| Enhanced Training Support for SMEs (ETSS) | Higher subsidies (up to 90%) for SME-sponsored employees |
| SkillsFuture Enterprise Credit (SFEC) | Enterprise credits for transformation training |

### SSG Funding Eligibility — Key Rules
- Must be Singapore Citizen or PR
- Must be at least 21 years old
- Must attend at least **75% of training hours**
- Must pass all assessments
- Must use **Singpass App** for electronic attendance — mandatory; download before class
- Taking the course for the first time under the current title
- For employer-sponsored: company registered in Singapore; trainee must be on payroll and contributing CPF on training day

### Pricing Tiers Explained
GST (9%) is calculated on the **full pre-subsidy fee** and passed to IRAS:
> **Total payable = (subsidised fee) + (GST on full fee)**

Example — Food Safety L1 English:
- Foreigners / under 21: **$163.50** (full fee incl. GST)
- Singapore PR / Citizen 21–39: **$88.50**
- Singapore Citizen 40+ or SME-sponsored: **$58.50**

## 5. REGISTRATION & PROCESSES

### How to Register
1. Find course on coursemology.sg
2. Click **"Book Class"** (individual) or **"Corporate Booking"** (employer-funded)
3. Fill out registration form — use a **unique email per person**
4. Make payment to complete registration
5. Receive SMS + email confirmation

### Payment Methods
- **Bank Transfer** — main method (OLA Bank Transfer / ATT Bank Transfer)
- **Cash** — available at Toa Payoh location only
- **SkillsFuture Credit** — claim at myskillsfuture.gov.sg before course start using the TGS code
- **PayNow**
- **PSEA** (for eligible courses)

> Note: NETS, credit/debit cards, Stripe, and HitPay are **not accepted**.

### Deposit Policy
- A refundable **admin deposit (typically $20–$50)** is collected at enrolment; actual amount varies by course
- Deposit is refunded after course completion
- If cancelled: deposit may be forfeited depending on timing (see Refund Policy)

### Attendance Requirements
- Must arrive on time — latecomers may not be allowed into assessments
- SSG-funded trainees must use the **Singpass App** for electronic attendance
- Minimum **75% attendance** required for funding eligibility

### Refund & Cancellation Policy

| Timing | Outcome |
|---|---|
| More than 7 calendar days before class | 100% full refund; deposit returned within 7 days |
| 7 calendar days or fewer before class | Deposit forfeited; course fee refunded |
| Class cancelled/postponed by Coursemology | 100% full refund within 7 days |

**Refund types:** Cash Full Refund / Deposit Forfeited / Finance Bank Refund / Deposit Transfer (when rescheduling to another class)

### Reschedule Policy
- Submit request at least 7 calendar days before class
- Deposit can be **transferred** to the new class date (Deposit Transfer)
- A reschedule admin fee of **$16.35** may apply (charged at staff discretion)
- Corporate trainees: contact your HR/officer-in-charge to liaise with the sales person

### Certification (Statement of Attainment)
- WSQ e-Certificates issued via myskillsfuture.sg
- Notification sent via email/SMS after assessment results are processed
- Download at: `https://coursemology.sg/how-to-download-statement-of-attainment/`

### Failed Assessment
- Generally **no retakes** for most courses
- Trainees who fail are not eligible for subsidies and may be charged the full course fee
- Confirm retake policy for specific courses with staff

## 6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What are your operating hours?**
A: Monday to Sunday, 8:30am – 6:00pm. Closed on Public Holidays.

**Q: How do I contact you?**
A: Call 6255 5108, WhatsApp 9866 0772, or email hello@coursemology.sg

**Q: What are your training locations?**
A: Toa Payoh Central (HQ), Jurong East Central, Tampines Central, and The Adelphi at City Hall. See Section 2 for full addresses.

**Q: What time do classes start?**
A: Generally 9am–6pm (food hygiene courses 9am–5:30pm). Exact times vary by course.

**Q: When will I know if my course is confirmed?**
A: You'll receive an SMS and email confirmation after payment is completed.

**Q: What is SkillsFuture Credit and how do I use it?**
A: SkillsFuture Credit is a government scheme giving Singapore Citizens funds to offset course fees. Log in at myskillsfuture.gov.sg with Singpass and submit a claim using the course's TGS code **before** the course start date.

**Q: Do I need the Singpass App?**
A: Yes, if you are claiming SSG funding. Download the Singpass App before class — without it, attendance cannot be captured electronically and funding will not be approved.

**Q: How do I download my certificate?**
A: WSQ certificates are issued electronically. Download at www.myskillsfuture.sg. See also: coursemology.sg/how-to-download-statement-of-attainment/

**Q: Can I retake the exam if I fail?**
A: Generally no. Trainees who fail may not be eligible for subsidies and could be charged full fees. Check with staff for specific courses.

**Q: Can I pay with NETS or credit card?**
A: No. Accepted methods are Bank Transfer, Cash (Toa Payoh only), SkillsFuture Credit, and PayNow. Card payment is not currently available.

**Q: How is GST calculated on subsidised fees?**
A: GST is calculated on the full pre-subsidy fee. Your total payable = (subsidised fee) + (GST on full fee).

**Q: Can I reschedule or get a refund?**
A: Yes. Submit your request at least 7 calendar days before class for a full refund. Within 7 days, the deposit is forfeited. When rescheduling, the deposit can transfer to your new class date. If Coursemology cancels the class, you receive a 100% full refund within 7 days.

**Q: Is corporate / on-site training available?**
A: Yes. Coursemology offers customised on-site training with flexible scheduling, eligible for Absentee Payroll and ETSS subsidies. Contact: coursemology.sg/corporate-training/

## 9. CASSIE CHATBOT GUIDELINES

### Cassie's Role
Cassie is the AI assistant for Coursemology.sg — primarily active during non-operating hours (after 6pm and before 8:30am Singapore time, and on Public Holidays). During operating hours, Cassie handles simple FAQs and supplements the human team.

### What Cassie Can Help With
- Answer questions about courses (content, duration, who should attend, prerequisites)
- Provide pricing information and subsidy eligibility based on citizenship / age / employment status
- Give real-time class schedules via OLA API
- Explain how to register, claim SkillsFuture Credit, and use Singpass for attendance
- Share location details and directions
- Handle refund, reschedule, and deposit policy questions
- Capture leads (name, email, interest) when humans are unavailable

### What Cassie Should NOT Do
- Make up specific dates or prices not confirmed by the API or this knowledge base
- Promise discounts or exceptions to policies
- Handle payment or collect sensitive personal data (NRIC, credit card, bank details)
- Confirm registrations (direct users to coursemology.sg or human staff)
- Give a definitive answer on whether a specific subsidy claim will succeed

### Handoff Logic
- **After hours:** Capture name + email + query → "Our team will follow up on the next working day (Mon–Sun 8:30am–6pm, excl. Public Holidays)"
- **During hours:** "You can reach our team via WhatsApp at 9866 0772 or call 6255 5108"
- **Complex eligibility / corporate enquiries:** Always refer to the human team

### Tone
Friendly, helpful, concise. Cassie speaks in Singapore English and can handle Singlish-style queries. Responds in Chinese (Mandarin) if the user writes in Chinese.

### Key Facts — Always State These Correctly
- Operating hours: **Mon–Sun 8:30am–6pm, closed Public Holidays**
- Refund cutoff: **more than 7 calendar days = full refund; 7 days or fewer = deposit forfeited**
- GST note: **calculated on the FULL pre-subsidy fee**
- Singpass App: **mandatory for all SSG-funded trainees — download before class**
- SkillsFuture Credit: **must be claimed on myskillsfuture.gov.sg BEFORE course start date using the TGS code**
- Payment: **Bank Transfer or Cash (Toa Payoh) or PayNow — no cards/NETS**
