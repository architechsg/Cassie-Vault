## Selling on Shopee — Ecommerce Platform, Marketing & Analytics (#CT41A)
- **OLA module_id:** 2130 | **TGS:** TGS-2021002777
- **Duration:** 3 days (24 hrs) | **Funding until:** 24 Feb 2027
- **Funding:** SFC eligible (WSQ)
- **Type:** WSQ

- **What you'll learn:** Many traditional SMEs and start-ups in Singapore face challenges in reaching customers and effectively selling online. Instead of investing significant time and resources into building and marketing a standalone website, businesses can leverage the existing customer base of established online platforms like Shopee. This Shopee course empowers participants to: Develop detailed e-commerce campaigns specifically tailored for online platforms such as Shopee, utilising their integrated marketing and analytical tools.; Gain the flexibility to focus on Shopee during the workshop, enabling them to establish real, actionable plans for their e-commerce presence immediately after the course.; Acquire the knowledge and strategies on how and where to ethically source the best products for their Shopee store, ensuring a competitive edge.;
- **Who should attend:** This Shopee seller course is ideal for: Anyone interested in leveraging the power of e-commerce to reach a wider customer base in Singapore.; Aspiring entrepreneurs and business owners looking to establish an online presence on Shopee.; Existing online sellers who wish to optimise their Shopee shop’s performance and marketing strategies; Individuals seeking to acquire in-demand e-commerce skills to enhance their career prospects.; Marketing professionals aiming to understand Shopee’s platform for effective digital campaigns.
- **Prerequisites:** Admission requirements: At least 1 GCE ‘N’ Level Pass; Have at least 1 year of working experience in a commercial company (preferably in sales and marketing); Be proficient users of either a Windows or Apple laptop; Be able to read, write and speak English at WSQ Workplace Literacy (WPL) Level 4; Be able to manipulate numbers at WSQ Workplace Numeracy (WPN) Level 4;
- **Assessment & certification:** Assessment Mode: Open-Book Summative; Assessment Methods and Duration: Practical Performance (Individual Project ) – 1 hour Written Assessment – 1 hour Participants who are assessed as Competent will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).

## Ecommerce Supply Chain, Warehousing, Logistics & Fulfilment (#CT42)
- **OLA module_id:** 2154 | **TGS:** TGS-2021003241
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hrs) | **Funding until:** 18 Mar 2027
- **Funding:** SFC eligible (WSQ)
- **Type:** WSQ

- **Course modules:** This comprehensive Ecommerce course delves deep into the operational backbone of any successful online business. You will learn practical knowledge and strategies across the entire supply chain, ensuring seamless product flow from supplier to customer. The modules are designed to give you a robust understanding of sourcing, importing, warehousing, and last-mile delivery within the Ecommerce landscape.
- **Prerequisites:** Learners are expected to: At least 1 GCE ‘N’ Level Pass; Have at least 1 year of working experience in a commercial company; Be able to read, write and speak English at WSQ Workplace Literacy (WPL) Level 4; Be able to manipulate numbers at WSQ Workplace Numeracy (WPN) Level 4 *Learners who do not qualify in terms of education but have the relevant working experience will be admitted on a case-by-case basis, subject to approval by the Management Representative (MR). Attire Comfortable and smart casual attire is recommended for this course.
- **Assessment & certification:** Assessment Mode: Open-Book Summative Assessment Methods & Duration: Practical Performance (Case Study Assessment) – 1.5 hour Written Assessment – 1 hour Participants who are assessed as Competent will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
