# Food Safety Course Level 3 — English (#AT31E)

Advanced WSQ course for food service supervisors, managers, and F&B owners. Trains participants to become certified **Food Hygiene Officers (FHOs)** — responsible for overseeing food safety compliance in their establishment.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 3 days (21 hours), 9am – 6pm
- **Target audience:** F&B supervisors, managers, business owners
- **Outcome:** Certified Food Hygiene Officer (FHO)

## What It Covers
- HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points) principles
- Food hygiene management systems
- Overseeing food safety compliance across an establishment

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** This course is designed to empower participants with the foundational knowledge and practical skills required to become a competent and certified Food Hygiene Officer in Singapore. Upon successful completion of this food safety course, you will be able to: Conduct comprehensive food safety and hygiene checks on personal hygiene, ingredient usage, food handling, storage, and premises cleanliness.; Understand and apply Pre-Requisite Programs and Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP) system principles to your daily operations.; Implement food safety and hygiene inspection policies, managing non-conformance through the Food Safety Management System (FSMS).; Certify your competency as an advanced food handler, preparing you to lead your team in maintaining a safe and hygienic food environment.
- **Course modules:** This food safety course is structured into key learning topics designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of advanced food hygiene and safety practices. The curriculum includes: Module 1: Introduction to Food Safety; Module 2: Food Safety Management Systems (FSMS); Module 3: Food Safety & Hygiene Auditing
- **Who should attend:** This advanced food safety course is designed for supervisors and managers in both retail and non-retail food establishments. Under the SAFE framework , certain food premises are required to appoint a senior staff member to be a trained and certified Food Hygiene Officer (FHO). This includes roles such as: Chief Chef; Sous Chef; Restaurant Manager; Operations Manager; Supervisor Important Pre-requisite Before attending this Level 3 course, all food handlers must have passed the WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1. This Level 3 course builds upon that foundational knowledge, preparing you for a supervisory role in maintaining rigorous food safety standards.
- **Prerequisites:** Participants must be in proper dress code and adhere to the following when attending the class: Wear covered shoes / sneakers with socks; Wear long pants; Have clean, short, trimmed nails; Keep long hair tied back and in a tidy condition The admission requirements are: At least 16 years old; At least 1 GCE ‘N’ Level Pass; Able to speak, listen, write, and read basic English; No prior work experience required
- **Assessment & certification:** At the end of the course, participants will be required to undergo a 2-hour assessment to demonstrate their competency. The assessment comprises: Practical Performance (PP); Written Assessment Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQ) Short-Answer Questions (SAQ) Upon completing all assessments and achieving a minimum attendance rate of 75%, learners will be awarded a Statement of Attainment (SOA) for Food Safety and Hygiene Level 3, issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG). This certification confirms your status as a competent Food Hygiene Officer (FHO). Unlike the basic Food Hygiene Certificate (Level 1), the Food Hygiene Officer certificate you obtain from this course is valid for a lifetime.
