# WSH for Food & Beverage Operations — Chinese, 8hrs (#AT13C)

Chinese-medium WSQ course for PWM-F&B compliance. Same content as English version.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 1 day (8 hours), 9am – 6pm

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** PWM-Food Services: Workplace Safety and Health for Food and Beverage Operations (8 hours) – Chinese 食品和饮料操作工作场所安全与健康中文课程 This Workplace Safety and Health for Food and Beverage Operations (Chinese) course will impart knowledge to learners on the importance of adhering to Workplace Safety and Health (WSH) policies and procedures and complying with them. This will help reduce the cases of workplace accidents in the food and beverage industry. 本食品和饮料操作工作场所安全与健康课程将向学习者传授遵守工作场所安全和健康（WSH）政策和程序并遵守这些政策和程序的重要性。这将有助于减少食品和饮料行业的工伤事故。 * PWM 所需课程（食品和饮料行业) / Course required for Progressive Wage Model (Food & Beverage Industry) * 如果要参加英文的, 请到 Workplace Safety and Health for Food and Beverage Operations (8 Hours) 课程名称 / Course Title PWM-Food Services: Workplace Safety and Health for Food and Beverage Operations (8 hours) – Chinese 课程目标 / Course Objective At the end of the course, learners will be able…
- **Prerequisites:** At least 18 years old / 至少18岁; Have minimum primary school education / 具有最低小学教育程度; Have a basic knowledge of the Singapore Workplace Safety and Health requirements / 具备新加坡工作场所安全和健康要求的基本知识; Able to speak, listen, write, and read English with Numeracy skills at a minimum proficiency level of the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification i.e., Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) Level 4 / 能够说、听、写和读英语，具备算术技能，最低熟练程度为就业能力技能劳动力技能资格，即工作场所识字和算术（WPLN）4级 Attire Wear Covered Shoes / Sneakers with socks; Wear Long/Full Pants; Have Clean, Short, Trimmed Nails; Tie up Medium/Long hair
- **Assessment & certification:** At the end of the course, participants will be required to undergo an assessment for 2 hours, which comprises of practical performance (PP), a written assessment that consists of Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQ), and Short Answer Questions (SAQ).
