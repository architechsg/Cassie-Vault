# Food Safety Course Level 1 — English (#AT11E)

SFA-accredited WSQ course for all food handlers in licensed food establishments. Formerly known as the Basic Food Hygiene Course. Mandatory for hawker stall operators, restaurant staff, food manufacturing workers, etc.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 1 day (7.5 hours), 9am – 5:30pm
- **Locations:** [[company/locations|Toa Payoh]], Jurong East, Tampines
- **Certificate:** WSQ Statement of Attainment (SOA), valid 5 years; renew at 5 years, then every 10 years
- **Accreditation:** SFA-accredited

## Pre-requisites
- Minimum 16 years old
- English literacy: WPL Level 4
- No open-toed shoes, jewellery, or nail polish on the day
- Long hair must be tied back

## Who Must Attend
All food handlers working in SFA-licensed food establishments (hawker centres, restaurants, food courts, food manufacturing). See [[concepts/wsq-explained]].

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** Upon successful completion of our course, participants will be certified as competent food handlers in Singapore. They will be equipped with the necessary skills to work safely and hygienically in retail and non-retail establishments in the Singapore food and beverage industry. This course was formerly known as the Basic Food Hygiene Course and was revised and renamed by the Singapore Food Agency (SFA) and SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) on 30 November 2020.
- **Course modules:** Modules covered in WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1 include: Module 1: Practise Good Personal Hygiene; Module 2: Use Safe Ingredients; Module 3: Handling Food Safely; Module 4: Store Food Safely; Module 5: Maintain cleanliness of utensils, equipment and service/storage areas
- **Who should attend:** Food handlers from both retail and non-retail food businesses They are required to pass the WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1. This includes: Individuals working in retail food establishments licensed by SFA (e.g. hawker stalls, food stalls, cafes, restaurants) who handle and prepare food and beverages.; Individuals working in non-retail food establishments such as food manufacturing and processing facilities. Examples: WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1 Kitchen managers Kitchen supervisors Chefs Sous chefs Cooks Kitchen assistants and food stall assistants Bartenders All food and beverage handlers.
- **Prerequisites:** Candidate must maintain proper dress code and adhere to all of the following pre-requisites when attending the class: Do not wear slippers or open-toed shoes; Do not wear accessories/jewellery; Do not wear nail polish or fake fingernails; Keep fingernails short and clean; Keep long hair tied back and in a neat and tidy condition Admission requirements: At least 16 years old; Able to speak, listen, write, and read basic English at WPL (Workplace Literacy) Level 4; Able to manipulate numbers at WPN (Workplace Numeracy) Level 4; No prior work experience required
- **Assessment & certification:** At the end of the course, participants will undergo a 1.5-hour assessment. This assessment comprises a Practical Performance (PP) and a Written Assessment. The Written Assessment includes both Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQ) and Short Answer Questions (SAQ). The assessment is estimated to take 1.5 hours in total. However, time taken to complete both components varies by learner. The assessment may end later than the stipulated time if necessary. Upon successful completion of all assessments, participants will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
