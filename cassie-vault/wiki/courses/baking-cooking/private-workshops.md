## Artisan Japanese Shio Pan Bread Workshop (#AT45)
- **OLA module_id:** 2464
- **Duration:** 1 day
- **Funding:** No subsidy.
- **Type:** Private

- **What you'll learn:** Home / Courses / Artisan Japanese Shio Pan Bread Workshop This hands‑on baking course introduces learners to the techniques required to produce soft, buttery Shio Pan with a crisp exterior. Participants will learn dough preparation, shaping, butter incorporation, and baking techniques to achieve consistent texture and flavour. Learn to make 5 Shio Pan flavours & fillings: Salted Butter Shio Pan; Caramel Miso Butter Shio Pan; Truffle Mushroom Butter Shio Pan; Garlic with Chilli Butter Shio Pan; Sun Dried Tomato Shio Pan Participants will be able to bring home the items prepared during the course. What You Bring Home Bring home the following: Your freshly made Shio Pans; A printed recipe in English & Chinese
- **Course modules:** This course covers the following key topics: Introduction to Japanese Shio Pan and key ingredients; Dough preparation, mixing, and fermentation techniques; Shaping, butter incorporation, and finishing methods; Baking techniques to achieve consistent texture and flavour; Cooling, presentation, and tasting
- **Who should attend:** This course is suitable for: Individuals and hobbyists; Aspiring home‑based or business learners; Corporate teams seeking hands‑on team‑bonding activities. Examples: Artisan Japanese Shio Pan Bread Workshop Home bakers and baking hobbyists Aspiring home‑based bakery owners Café or bakery assistants Food & beverage enthusiasts Corporate teams participating in team‑bonding workshops
- **Prerequisites:** Aged 18 and above.; Basic literacy and numeracy skills. Attire It is advisable to wear long pants and covered shoes as you will be standing for a long period.
- **Assessment & certification:** Not applicable. Training is conducted in a classroom setting with hands‑on practice. Learners will work at fully equipped stations and observe live demonstrations by in‑house chefs covering the complete baking process.
