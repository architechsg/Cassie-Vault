# Bake Artisan and Decorative Breads (#AT43)

WSQ course on artisan bread-baking techniques including decorative shaping.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 4 days (32 hours), 9am – 6pm

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** Upon completion, participants can expect to be able to: Follow food hygiene standards during bread-making; Assemble and use baking tools and equipment properly; Select and prepare ingredients for specific recipes; Mix, ferment, shape, proof, and bake artisan and decorative breads; Test for doneness, de-pan, and decorate breads; Maintain a clean and hygienic workstation; Understand dough processes and adjust formulas for lean and enriched breads; Identify and prevent common baking faults; Create and maintain a sourdough starter
- **Course modules:** This course will equip learners with the knowledge and skills to prepare a variety of artisan breads commonly enjoyed worldwide. Learners will acquire this expertise through a combination of hands-on practical training and classroom learning: French Baguette – Classic crusty French bread; Focaccia – Italian flatbread infused with olive oil and herbs; Ciabatta – Rustic Italian bread with airy texture; Starter Dough for Sourdough – Basics of sourdough fermentation; Rustic Country Loaf – Hearty bread with chewy crumb; Sourdough Bread – Naturally fermented bread with tangy flavor; Pullman Loaf (Poolish Method) – Square-shaped bread ideal for sandwiches; Bagel – Chewy, boiled bread with glossy crust
- **Who should attend:** This SkillsFuture baking course is ideal for: Those wanting to learn the skills to make sourdough bread in a structured environment.; Anyone wanting to learn bread-making with SkillsFuture credits.; Individuals seeking to learn the fundamentals of artisan bread-making in Singapore.; Home bakers looking to enhance their skills and knowledge.; Aspiring bakers aiming to pursue a career in the food industry. Examples: WSQ Bake Artisan and Decorative Breads Course Aspiring and junior bakers Bakery or café staff F&B professionals Baking enthusiasts
- **Prerequisites:** Aged 18 and above.; Basic literacy and numeracy skills.; Interest in baking. Attire It is advisable to wear long pants and covered shoes as you will be standing for a long period.
- **Assessment & certification:** Not Applicable. The training is conducted in the classroom, just like in baking school. Each learner will have their own fully equipped station, where they can try their hand at making several recipes. There will also be a live demonstration, where in-house chefs will be teaching and demonstrating the entire process live. Participants who fulfil all requirements will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG). This certification confirms your completion of a comprehensive baking course in Singapore.
