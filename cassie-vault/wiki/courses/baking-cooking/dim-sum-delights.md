# Dim Sum Delights (#AT41)

WSQ course covering traditional dim sum preparation techniques.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours), 9am – 6pm

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or curious beginner, our hands-on cooking class will guide you through the intricate techniques of Dim Sum making so you can confidently recreate these popular dishes at home or in the workplace. Upon completion of this Dim Sum making course, you will be able to: Assemble the tools and equipment required for dim sum preparation; Prepare ingredients for different types of dim sum dishes; Combine ingredients into the dim sum items to be cooked; Cook different dim sum dishes at the required temperatures using appropriate techniques across the required time intervals; Apportion, plate, and store finished products and ingredients appropriately; Restore workstations after cooking Come and learn the art of making that delicious and favorite Dim Sum of yours, in one of the best Dim Sum courses in Singapore.
- **Course modules:** Chinese Dim Sum Making Course This course will equip learners with the knowledge and skills to prepare an assortment of basic Dim Sum dishes commonly seen in Chinese restaurants. Learners will acquire this expertise in preparing moist-heat Dim Sum dishes through a combination of hands-on practical training and classroom learning: Har Gow (Shrimp Dumplings) 虾饺 – Shrimp wrapped in translucent tapioca skin; Hong Kong Style Egg Tarts 蛋橽 – Baked pastry crust with egg custard filling; Salted Egg Custard Bun 奶皇包 – Steam bun with salted egg custard filling; Siew Mai (Chicken and Shrimp Dumpling) 烧卖 – Steamed dumpling with chicken & shrimp filling; Chee Cheong Fun (Rice noodle rolls) 豬腸粉 – Steamed rice rolls Learners will also get to bring home the above items.
- **Who should attend:** This course is ideal for: Existing kitchen staff looking to enhance their skills in Dim Sum preparation.; Food establishments seeking to train their employees instead of hiring new staff.; Aspiring cooks who want to start a career in the culinary industry.; Individuals with no prior experience interested in learning the fundamentals of Dim Sum making.; Business owners looking to expand their menu with authentic Dim Sum offerings..
- **Prerequisites:** Learners should be at least 18 years old.; Learners should have at least primary school or equivalent education.; Learners must be able to listen, speak, read, and write English at a level equivalent to Workplace Literacy – Level 4 and above.; Learners who do not meet the above requirements will be required to go through a short interview to verify their language and cognitive ability. Attire It is advisable to wear long pants and covered shoes as you will be standing for a long period.
- **Assessment & certification:** WA: Written Assessment – 30 minutes; PA: Practical Assessment – 1 hour; Total duration: 1 hour and 30 minutes Attendance requirement: min 75% attendance; Course completion requirements: Fulfil at least 75% minimum attendance and pass assessments – competent (Refer to mode of assessment for details); Participants who fulfill all requirements will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG). The SOA will be reflected as FSS-FBS-1006-1.1 Chinese Moist-Heat Dish Preparation and Cooking-1. Validity The Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore is a lifetime certificate with no expiry date and does not require renewal.
