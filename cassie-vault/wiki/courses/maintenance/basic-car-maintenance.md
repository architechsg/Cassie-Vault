# Basic Car Maintenance with Hands-On (#AT22A)

Practical WSQ course covering basic car maintenance tasks with hands-on practice. Suitable for car owners who want to maintain their own vehicles.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 1 day (8 hours), 9am – 6pm
- **Class size:** Minimum 3 pax, maximum 10 pax per class

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** This car maintenance course aims to equip participants with the essential skills and knowledge to perform preventive maintenance, conduct serviceability checks, and carry out first-level corrective maintenance on Class 3 vehicles. Participants will gain a practical understanding of vehicle systems and learn how to identify and address common issues. Course Outcomes Our car mechanic course is conducted at a real-life car workshop in Ubi, Singapore, offering invaluable hands-on experience with an actual car. This practical approach ensures you gain confidence in managing your vehicle’s health.
- **Course modules:** Car Mechanic (Basic Maintenance with Hands-on) This car mechanic course in Singapore provides foundational skills for vehicle upkeep, ensuring you gain practical knowledge for routine maintenance and problem-solving. Morning Session (9am to 1pm): Class Theory Afternoon Session (2pm to 6pm): Hands-On Practical
- **Who should attend:** This basic car maintenance course is ideal for: Anyone interested in gaining confidence in diagnosing common car problems and ensuring vehicle longevity.; New and experienced car owners in Singapore who wish to understand their vehicle better and perform basic upkeep.; Individuals aiming to reduce dependency on mechanics for routine checks and minor repairs.; Aspiring automotive enthusiasts looking for an entry-level car mechanic course to kickstart their knowledge..
- **Prerequisites:** At least 16 years old.; Have minimum primary school education.; Have a basic knowledge of the Singapore Workplace Safety and Health requirements.; Able to speak, listen, write, and read English with Numeracy skills at a minimum proficiency level of the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification i.e., Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) Level 3.; No prior maintenance/technical work experience required. Attire Comfortable and practical clothing suitable for a workshop environment.; Closed-toe shoes are mandatory for safety reasons.
- **Assessment & certification:** This car maintenance course is conducted in person at a real-life car workshop, providing an immersive and hands-on learning experience. Practical Assessment – Conducted on Day 1; Written Assessment – MCQs and SAQs Upon successful completion of all requirements for this automotive course, participants will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
