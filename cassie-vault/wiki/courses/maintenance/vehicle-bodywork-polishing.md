# Vehicle Body Work Maintenance with Polishing (#AT25)

WSQ course covering vehicle bodywork care and polishing techniques.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours), 9am – 6pm
- **Class size:** Minimum 3 pax, maximum 10 pax per class

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** This hands-oncourse will equip learners with the essential skills and knowledge to effectively carry out preventive maintenance, serviceability checks, and servicing on the vehicle body. It is an entry-level course that is also suitable for learners who are interested in picking up vehicle bodywork maintenance know-how. Course Outcomes At the end of the course, learners will be able to: LO1: Describe the types of vehicle components and parts that require maintenance; LO2: Identify the types of maintenance supplies, spare parts, and consumables for vehicle bodywork maintenance; LO3: Set up tools, equipment, materials, and checklists required for preventive maintenance tasks; LO4: Conduct serviceability checks and inspections on vehicle bodywork;
- **Who should attend:** This vehicle bodywork maintenance with polishing hands-on course is ideal for: Anyone interested in learning basic vehicle bodywork care and polishing techniques; New and experienced car owners who want to maintain their vehicle’s appearance; Individuals aiming to reduce reliance on workshops for minor bodywork upkeep; Automotive hobbyists looking to gain practical skills for personal use. Examples: Vehicle Body Work Maintenance (with Hands-on) Car owners / new drivers Delivery drivers Fleet or operations staff Sales staff who use company vehicles Workshop assistants Ride-hailing or private-hire drivers
- **Prerequisites:** At least 16 years old.; Have minimum primary school education.; Have a basic knowledge of the Singapore Workplace Safety and Health requirements.; Able to speak, listen, write, and read English with Numeracy skills at a minimum proficiency level of the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification i.e., Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) Level 3.; No prior maintenance/technical work experience required.
- **Assessment & certification:** Upon successful completion of all requirements for this automotive course, participants will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
