# Advanced Air-Con Maintenance (#AT24)

Advanced WSQ course for deeper air-conditioning maintenance skills, building on the basic course.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** By the end of the course, participants will be able to: Identify corrective maintenance tasks from work orders and technical specifications; Diagnose system faults using advanced troubleshooting procedures and error code tables; Apply safety procedures when using specialised tools and high‑pressure equipment; Carry out professional repair, replacement, and system reinstatement works; Validate system performance through functional testing and documentation
- **Who should attend:** This course is suitable for individuals who already have basic air‑conditioning servicing knowledge and hands‑on experience. Examples: Air‑conditioning technicians HVAC service and maintenance technicians Building maintenance technicians Facilities maintenance personnel Technical officers handling air‑con systems
- **Prerequisites:** At least 16 years old; Have a minimum primary school education; Have a basic knowledge of the Singapore Workplace Safety and Health requirements; Able to speak, listen, write, and read English with Numeracy skills at a minimum proficiency level of the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification, i.e., Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) Level 3; Have a keen interest in how air conditioning systems work, and be willing to participate in practical hands-on work in semi-outdoor areas;
- **Assessment & certification:** Assessment will be conducted through: Practical hands‑on performance assessment; Demonstration of correct diagnostic, repair, and testing procedures; Completion of required maintenance documentationment Participants who are assessed as Competent will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
