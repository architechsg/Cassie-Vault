# Basic Air-Con Maintenance with Hands-On (#AT23)

Practical WSQ course on basic air-conditioning maintenance for homeowners and property managers.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 1 day (8 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** At the end of this aircon maintenance and servicing course, learners will be able to: Describe the types, functions, and operating principles of air-conditioning and mechanical ventilation systems and their components.; Perform preparation work to conduct preventive maintenance on air-conditioning systems.; Perform serviceability and installation checks on air-conditioning systems.; Perform preventive maintenance and servicing in accordance with organisational maintenance procedures and/or technical manuals and safety guidelines.; Record maintenance activities and report faults identified.
- **Who should attend:** This hands-on heavy course will equip learners with the essential skills and knowledge to effectively carry out preventive maintenance, serviceability checks and servicing on air-conditioning systems. It is an entry-level course that is also suitable for learners who are interested in picking up air-con maintenance know-how. Beginners and non-technical learners; Homeowners looking to save on maintenance; Maintenance personnel and handymen; Helpers or assistants in facilities; Anyone wanting hands-on experience with basic aircon systems.
- **Prerequisites:** At least 16 years old; Have a minimum primary school education; Have a basic knowledge of the Singapore Workplace Safety and Health requirements; Able to speak, listen, write, and read English with Numeracy skills at a minimum proficiency level of the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification, i.e., Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) Level 3; Have a keen interest in how air conditioning systems work, and be willing to participate in practical hands-on work in semi-outdoor areas;
- **Assessment & certification:** This course is conducted in person, in a classroom setting, providing an interactive and engaging learning experience with Practical Hands-on. Assessment Mode: Open-Book Summative; Assessment Methods and Duration: Practical Performance: 0.5 hour Written Assessment: 0.5 hour Participants who are assessed as Competent will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
