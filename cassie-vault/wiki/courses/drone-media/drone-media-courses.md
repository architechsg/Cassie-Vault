## WSQ Aerial Photography and Videography with Drones (#CT11)
- **OLA module_id:** 2110 | **TGS:** TGS-2022012851
- **Duration:** 4 days (32 hrs)
- **Note:** Funding period ended Jul 2025 — **confirm current subsidy status with staff before booking**
- **Type:** WSQ
- **Course structure:** Day 1 = theory (at Toa Payoh training centre). Days 2–4 = practical flying sessions at **Sin Ming Open Field** (not at the training centre). Students are added to a WhatsApp group by the trainer to coordinate practical session logistics. Bring NRIC/FIN and SingPass on all days.

- **What you'll learn:** This course aims to equip participants with a clear understanding of drone flying principles, safe operating practices, and regulatory requirements in Singapore. Participants will learn how to operate drones confidently for aerial photography and videography purposes, while developing the skills to capture high‑quality aerial photos and videos. The course also introduces basic aerial cinematography techniques to help participants enhance the visual quality of their drone footage.
- **Course modules:** WSQ Drone Photography & Aerial Videography Carry out aerial shoots using drones; Capture aerial sequences according to technical and creative directions; Conduct planning and effective execution of shoots Legal Considerations Operational Considerations Safety Considerations
- **Who should attend:** This WSQ Drone Photography & Aerial Videography Course is ideal for: Individuals interested in learning to operate drones for aerial photography and videography.; New or aspiring drone users in Singapore seeking to understand safe flying practices and regulations.; Professionals looking to create high‑quality aerial photos and videos in‑house.; Photography and videography enthusiasts transitioning into aerial cinematography..
- **Prerequisites:** At least 1 GCE ‘N’ Level Pass; At least 1 year of working experience in any capacity; Able to speak, listen and read English at a proficiency level not lower than the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification Workplace Literacy Level (WPL Level 3)
- **Assessment & certification:** Upon successful completion of all requirements for this automotive course, participants will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).

## WSQ Aerial Surveillance with Drones — Pre UAPL (#CT12A)
- **OLA module_id:** 2316 | **TGS:** TGS-2022012852
- **Duration:** 4 days (32 hrs)
- **Funding:** SFC eligible (WSQ) — confirm funding period with staff
- **Type:** WSQ

- **What you'll learn:** This course aims to equip trainees with the knowledge and practical skills to set up and operate drones for aerial surveillance in compliance with CAAS regulations. Participants will learn to operate drones safely, manoeuvre aerial camera systems according to flight plans, and produce aerial captures that meet technical and operational requirements. Course Outcomes Upon successful completion of the course, trainees will be able to: Set up drones and aerial camera systems for surveillance operations in compliance with CAAS regulations; Operate drones safely and competently for aerial surveillance purposes; Produce aerial surveillance footage that meets technical and operational requirements; Execute flight plans and manoeuvre drones according to surveillance objectives; Complete relevant documentation for remote drone surveillance operations;
- **Course modules:** Theory and Practical Sessions for Participants to learn: O1: Describe the different types of drones and functionalities used in security surveillance today; LO2: Complete relevant documentation relating to remote drone surveillance operations; LO3: Set up drones for surveillance purposes; LO4: Operate drones for surveillance purposes; LO5: Maintain the drones and their detachable surveillance equipment; LO6: Escalate abnormal behaviours discovered to appropriate authorities and enforcement teams.
- **Who should attend:** This WSQ Aerial Surveillance Course is ideal for: Individuals who want to build foundation skills in aerial drone operations , especially for surveillance or inspection tasks.; Learners preparing for more advanced drone licensing (e.g., Unmanned Aerial Pilot Licence – UAPL) with relevant authorities. Examples: WSQ Aerial Surveillance Course Security and surveillance officers Facilities and operations management staff Built environment and infrastructure professionals involved in site monitoring Safety, inspection, and compliance personnel Individuals with a professional interest in aerial surveillance and drone‑based monitoring
- **Prerequisites:** Admission requirements: At least 1 GCE ‘N’ Level Pass; At least 1 year of working experience in any capacity; Able to speak, listen and read English at a proficiency level not lower than the Employability Skills Workforce Skills Qualification Workplace Literacy Level (WPL Level 3) *Learners who do not qualify in terms of education but have the relevant working experience will be admitted on a case-by-case basis, subject to approval by the Management Representative (MR).
- **Assessment & certification:** Upon successful completion of all assessments, participants will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) and and should proceed to 2nd course to learn more on Drones
