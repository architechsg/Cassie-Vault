# Automation Management — Developing Generative AI Chatbots (#AT52)

WSQ course on building and deploying generative AI chatbots for business automation.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to: Identify the strengths, limitations, and features of popular no-code AI chatbot platforms like Microsoft Copilot and Copilot Studio.; Evaluate the benefits, trade-offs, and business impact of integrating AI chatbots into customer service operations.; Design and build functional, user-friendly chatbots optimized for real-world service delivery using no-code tools.; Integrate chatbots with existing company websites, CRMs, and digital platforms for seamless operations.; Monitor chatbot performance and apply iterative improvements to enhance service outcomes.
- **Course modules:** (Generative AI Chatbots) This course will cover the following topics: Understanding AI Chatbot Platforms: An introduction to the features, strengths, and limitations of popular no-code platforms used for building AI chatbots.; Analyzing Business Fit and Impact: Learn to evaluate the trade-offs and benefits of integrating an AI chatbot into business operations and its potential impact on customer service.; Designing Functional Chatbots: Gain skills in designing and optimizing a functional chatbot that can handle real-world service delivery in a user-friendly manner.; Building a No-Code Chatbot: Hands-on practice in building a chatbot from the ground up using Microsoft Copilot Studio without the need for coding.;
- **Who should attend:** This course is ideal for: Business & Operations Managers; Customer Service Leaders; Automation & Digital Transformation Teams; IT & Product Managers; Business Owners using AI chatbots. Examples: Automation Management (Generative AI Chatbots) Operations Manager Customer Service Manager Automation Specialist IT Manager Product Manager Digital Transformation Lead Business Owner
- **Prerequisites:** At least a GCE ‘N’ Level graduate or a Workplace Literacy Level 5 or higher; At least one year of working experience in a role that uses computers at work; Learners who do not meet the education qualification requirements but have at least 3 years of relevant working experience can be admitted on a case-by-case basis subject to approval by the Management Representative.
- **Assessment & certification:** Classroom-Based Training; Duration: 2 Days (16 hours – including assessment) Generative AI Tools Used Copilot Studio: A dedicated no-code platform used to build, deploy, and manage AI-powered chatbots.; Copilot: An AI assistant integrated into Microsoft apps that helps boost productivity.; ChatGPT: A foundational language model from OpenAI that excels at generating human-like text and powers AI assistants like ChatGPT.; Claude: A family of AI models from Anthropic designed for safety and reliability, excelling at thoughtful dialogue and detailed content creation.;
