# Workflow Efficiency with Generative AI Technologies (#CT51)

WSQ course on applying generative AI tools to improve business workflow efficiency.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** Improving Work Processes with Generative AI Technologies Home / Courses / Enhancing Workflow Efficiency with Generative AI Solutions Enhancing Workflow Efficiency with Generative AI Solutions empowers professionals to use Generative AI tools to improve daily work efficiency and business processes. This course equips non‑IT professionals, managers, and executives with practical skills to identify workflow challenges, apply Generative AI tools for writing, research, data analysis, and productivity improvement, and develop proposals to support AI adoption within organisations. Learners will gain a structured approach to evaluating AI opportunities and integrating suitable solutions to streamline operations and enhance workplace productivity.
- **Course modules:** Learning Unit 1: Workflow Analysis and Technological Gap Identification Identify workflow and system inefficiencies; Distinguish process issues versus system or software issues; Develop clear, actionable problem statements Learning Unit 2: Monitoring Trends and Best Practices in Generative AI Understand key Generative AI trends relevant to business; Identify reliable sources for AI technology updates; Evaluate best practices in AI adoption Learning Unit 3: Employing Generative AI Solutions Apply Generative AI tools to support productivity and efficiency; Use AI for content creation, research, and analysis tasks; Measure improvements in work outcomes and efficiency Learning Unit 4: Driving Adoption of AI Solutions Build a business case for Generative AI adoption; Identify relevant success metrics and considerations; Develop structured proposals for management approval
- **Who should attend:** This course is ideal for: Business managers and team leaders; Operations and process improvement professionals; Executives and decision‑makers; Project managers; IT and digital transformation team members; Entrepreneurs and startup founders
- **Prerequisites:** Basic proficiency in using computers and common workplace digital tools; Experience working in an organisational or business environment; Interest in improving work processes and productivity using technology
