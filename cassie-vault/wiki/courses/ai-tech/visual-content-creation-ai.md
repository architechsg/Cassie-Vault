# Business Visual Content Creation with Generative AI Tools (#AT53)

WSQ course on using AI tools to create visual content for business purposes.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** This course provides the relevant knowledge and skills needed to create and manage business-oriented visual content using Generative AI tools effectively and ethically. Upon completion, participants will be able to: Understand and apply principles of AI ethics and copyright in content creation.; Facilitate research into emerging content creation methods and tools.; Manage concept creation and partnerships with external content creators and vendors to achieve content requirements.; Produce the required content in alignment with stakeholders’ requirements using AI Tools.; Liaise with relevant stakeholders during the testing and implementation of content to ensure that content is aligned with their artistic vision.
- **Course modules:** opic 1: Emerging Trends and Trending GenAI Tools in Visual Content Creation Topic 2: AI Ethics and Copyright Topic 3: How to Develop Concept and Ideas for your Still Images and Video Project Using GenAI Tools Partnerships with External AI Content Creators and Vendors Topic 4: Smartphone Photography and Videography Techniques Camera Basics; Capturing the Perfect Angle Topic 5: Basics of Prompting for AI Tools What Is Prompting and Why Is It Important In AI?; Crafting Clear and Effective Prompts for Different AI Tools Topic 6: Best Practices for Optimizing Results From AI-Generated Content Topic 7: Image Generation with GenAI Tools ChatGPT; Leonardo.ai; Microsoft Designer; Canva Topic 8: Video Creation Using TikTok on the Smartphone Video Creation Process; Video Creation Technique;
- **Who should attend:** Marketing and Social Media Professionals; Business Owners and Entrepreneurs; Content Creators and Digital Marketers; Communication and Public Relations Specialists; Individuals interested in leveraging AI for creative or business purposes
- **Prerequisites:** Admission requirements: At least a GCE ‘N’ Level graduate or a Workplace Literacy Level 5 or higher; At least one year of working experience in a role that uses computers at work; Learners who do not meet the education qualification requirements, but have at least 3 years of relevant working experience can be admitted on a case-by-case basis subject to approval by the Management Representative
- **Assessment & certification:** Upon successful completion of all assessments, participants will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) issued by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG).
