# AI-Powered PowerPoint — Tech-Aided Business Presentation (#AT54)

WSQ course on using AI tools to create and deliver effective business presentations in PowerPoint.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (18 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** AI-Powered Powerpoint Business Presentation Home / Courses / AI-Powered Powerpoint Business Presentation Delivering an engaging business presentation requires more than good slides — it demands insight, clarity, and confidence. This course equips professionals with the tools to research their audience using generative AI, create impactful visual presentations, and deliver messages that resonate. Through hands-on activities and real-world scenarios, learners will build, present, and refine their presentations using best practices in design and delivery.
- **Course modules:** Business Presentation Delivery with PowerPoint LU1: Target Audience Profiling AI tools for audience research (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini) Crafting effective prompts and insights analysis; LU2: Presentation Deck Creation & Delivery Visual design (charts, infographics, images, animations) Storyboarding and structuring slides (Hook – Problem – Solution – CTA) Enhancing engagement through interactivity and visuals; LU3: Continuous Improvements from Feedback Collecting and analyzing feedback Lessons learned slide creation and revision strategies
- **Who should attend:** This course is ideal for: Anyone looking to improve presentation skills with technology tools; Professionals involved in client presentations, training, or internal updates; Business development, sales, and marketing teams; HR professionals conducting training or pitching initiatives. Examples: AI-Powered Powerpoint Business Presentation Sales Executives & Business Development Managers Marketing Professionals Trainers & Facilitators Corporate Communications Specialists Project Managers & Team Leads HR Practitioners Consultants & Client-Facing Professionals
- **Prerequisites:** Familiarity with Microsoft PowerPoint recommended; Basic computer literacy; Language: Workplace Literacy and Numeracy Level 2 (WPLN: Speaking, Writing, Listening, reading, and Numeracy at ESS level 2).
- **Assessment & certification:** Classroom-Based Training; Duration: 2 Days (16 hours – including assessment) Generative AI Tools Used ChatGPT (OpenAI): a highly conversational tool known for its powerful natural language generation. It excels at simulating personas and supports detailed prompt engineering, making it ideal for exploring scenario-based insights. Furthermore, it benefits from being a widely supported and recognized platform.; Gemini (Google): stands out with its multi-modal capabilities, adeptly handling visual prompts and referencing real-time data.
