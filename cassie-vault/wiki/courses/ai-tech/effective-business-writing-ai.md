# Effective Business Writing with Generative AI Tools (#AT51)

WSQ course teaching professionals how to use generative AI tools to enhance business writing quality and efficiency.

## Key Details
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)

## Course Content


- **What you'll learn:** Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to: Define the required flow, structure, and content for various business reports and communications.; Leverage generative AI tools to create effective and persuasive business documents.; Tailor messages to different audiences for better engagement and impact.; Review and refine drafted communications for clarity, tone, completeness, and accuracy with the help of AI.; Apply feedback to continuously improve the effectiveness of their business writing.
- **Course modules:** Effective Business Writing Supported by Generative AI Tools LO1. Define the required flow, structure and contents of reports and other business communiques; LO2. Create effective business reports and communiques with the help of generative AI tools •; LO3. Review drafted communiques for sufficiency of coverage and completeness with the help of generative AI tools; LO4. Edit drafted communiques to minimise errors with the help of generative AI tools; LO5. Submit finalised reports and communiques to relevant stakeholders; LO6.
- **Who should attend:** This course is ideal for: Business professionals; Managers, team leads, and coordinators; Marketing, communications, and HR staff; Anyone wanting to use AI tools. Examples: Effective Business Writing Supported by Generative AI Tools Business executives & managers Team leads & project coordinators Marketing, communications & HR professionals Sales & business development staff Administrative & operations staff who prepare reports or presentations
- **Prerequisites:** At least a GCE ‘N’ Level graduate or a Workplace Literacy Level 5 or higher; At least one year of working experience in a role that uses computers at work; Learners who do not meet the education qualification requirements but have at least 3 years of relevant working experience can be admitted on a case-by-case basis subject to approval by the Management Representative.
- **Assessment & certification:** Classroom-Based Training; Duration: 2 Days (16 hours – including assessment) AI Tools Used Perplexity: A research-focused tool for quickly finding accurate, cited information to support your reports and business documents. ChatGPT: A versatile writing assistant for brainstorming ideas, refining drafts, and structuring professional documents. Gemini: A multimodal tool used to analyze audience needs and generate insights for creating targeted messaging. You will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) from SkillsFuture Singapore upon successful completion of this module and its assessment
