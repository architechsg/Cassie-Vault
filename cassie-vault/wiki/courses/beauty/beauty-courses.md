## Professional Hair Cutting and Styling Essentials — WSQ (#AB81)
- **OLA module_id:** 2454 | **TGS:** TGS-2025060725 | Provider: Associates Consulting Pte Ltd
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hrs) | **Funding until:** 1 Dec 2027
- **Funding:** SFC eligible (WSQ)
- **Type:** WSQ

- **What you'll learn:** This intensive two-day programme provides aspiring and current hair professionals with comprehensive expertise in modern hair cutting and styling techniques. Participants will delve into the nuances of design conceptualisation, master precision cutting methods, and explore diverse styling applications. Unique to this course is its emphasis on client-centric approaches, ensuring graduates can expertly assess individual needs, adapt to varying hair types, and execute designs that enhance natural features, all while adhering to the highest standards of safety and professionalism. Course Title Professional Hair Cutting and Styling Essentials This course will take place in person, in a classroom. Professional Hair Cutting and Styling Essentials course is available in English
- **Prerequisites:** 1 year of working experience in any sector; and; Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) 4 or equivalent; *Learners without the right WPLN or equivalent qualifications can be accepted if they have at least 3 years of relevant working experience in beauty or media-related roles, subject to the MR’s approval.
- **Assessment & certification:** You will receive a Statement of Attainment (SOA) from SkillsFuture Singapore upon successful completion of this module and its assessment

## Professional Makeup Essentials — WSQ (#AB82)
- **OLA module_id:** 2470 | **TGS:** TGS-2026063246 | Provider: Associates Consulting Pte Ltd
- **Duration:** 2 days (16 hrs) | **Funding period:** Apr 2026 – Apr 2028 (confirm with staff)
- **Funding:** SFC eligible (WSQ)
- **Type:** WSQ

- **What you'll learn:** Participants will learn to: Maintain hygiene and safety standards; Prepare skin for make-up application; Use essential tools and products correctly; Apply foundation, concealer, and coverage techniques; Shape eyebrows and apply eye makeup (eyeshadow, eyeliner, mascara); Apply false eyelashes and blusher for different face shapes; Reshape lips and apply lipstick and lip liner; Provide basic aftercare advice
- **Course modules:** By the end of this course, you will be able to: LO1 : Implement thorough client consultation and workstation sanitation procedures prior to any cosmetic service; LO2 : Select and apply suitable cosmetic products and methodologies after assessing facial features and skin conditions; LO3 : Apply foundational makeup techniques, adhering strictly to industry best practices and client specifications; LO4 : Customise makeup designs and colour harmonies to complement a spectrum of occasions and artistic briefs; LO5 : Apply advanced correctional and perfecting techniques to achieve the desired look; LO6 : Ensure the durability and impeccable presentation of makeup, accounting for environmental variables and wear time; LO7 : Offer advice on post-makeup care and maintenance
- **Who should attend:** This Basic Makeup course is ideal for: Individuals interested in personal grooming and beauty skills; Beginners who want to learn basic make-up techniques for themselves or others; Beauty enthusiasts looking to enhance their knowledge of skincare and make-up application; Students or hobbyists exploring make-up as a creative skill; Anyone seeking confidence in applying everyday or special occasion make-up.
- **Prerequisites:** 1 year of working experience in any sector; and; Workplace Literacy and Numeracy (WPLN) 4 or equivalent *Learners without the right WPLN or equivalent qualifications can be accepted if they have at least 3 years of relevant working experience in beauty or media-related roles, subject to the MR’s approval.”
- **Assessment & certification:** Participants who fulfil all requirements will be certified with a Certificate issued by Associates Consulting Pte Ltd. Validity The SkillsFuture certification from this Basic Makeup class does not require renewal.
