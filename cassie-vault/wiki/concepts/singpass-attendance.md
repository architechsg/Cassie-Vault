# Singpass App & Electronic Attendance

## Why You Need It
SSG requires **electronic attendance** for all government-subsidised courses. The Singpass App is the tool used to capture this. Without it, your attendance cannot be recorded electronically and SSG funding will not be approved.

## Who Needs It
All trainees claiming:
- SSG subsidies (MCES pricing)
- SkillsFuture Credit
- UTAP (NTUC)
- Any other government-funded training support

Private/non-subsidised trainees do not need Singpass for attendance.

## What to Do
1. Download the **Singpass App** before your class day (App Store / Google Play)
2. Set up your Singpass login if you haven't already
3. On class day, use the app when prompted by the trainer to scan in/out

## Common Issue
Students who forget to bring or set up Singpass on the day may not have their attendance recorded. This can result in losing SSG funding eligibility for that session. There is no workaround after the fact — set it up in advance.
