# What Information You Need to Register for a WSQ Course

For WSQ (SkillsFuture) courses, Coursemology is required to collect specific personal information to submit your grant claim to SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG). This is a government requirement — without it, SSG funding cannot be approved.

## Required Information at Registration

Have the following ready when filling out the registration form:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Full name** | Must match your NRIC / FIN / Passport exactly |
| **NRIC / FIN / Passport number** | Singapore Citizens & PRs: NRIC. Work pass holders: FIN. Others: Passport |
| **Date of birth** | As per your identity document |
| **Nationality** | |
| **Gender** | |
| **Race** | Required for SSG records |
| **Singapore address** | Residential or correspondence address |
| **Mobile number** | |
| **Email address** | Must be unique per person — you cannot share an email with another registrant |
| **Job designation / occupation** | Your current job title |
| **Monthly salary** | Required for SSG subsidy assessment |
| **Highest education level** | e.g. Primary, Secondary, Diploma, Degree |

## Why Is This Collected?

Coursemology submits this information to SSG via the TP-Gateway portal to claim government subsidies on your behalf. Your data is handled in accordance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

If your information is incomplete or incorrect, the SSG grant claim may be rejected — which could mean you are charged the full (unsubsidised) course fee.

## For SkillsFuture Credit (SFC) Claims

If you are using your SkillsFuture Credit, you additionally need:
- Your **SkillsFuture Credit claim reference number** (generated when you submit the claim on myskillsfuture.sg)
- The claim must be submitted **before the class starts** using the course's TGS code

See [[funding/skillsfuture-credit]] for step-by-step instructions.

## For SME / Corporate-Sponsored Trainees

Your employer handles the corporate booking and provides the company details (UEN, company name). You still need to supply your own personal data listed above — the company cannot submit on your behalf without it.

## Not Applicable for Private / Non-Funded Courses

For non-WSQ private workshops (e.g. private cooking workshops, Sake Appreciation), the information requirements are simpler — name, mobile, and email are typically sufficient.
