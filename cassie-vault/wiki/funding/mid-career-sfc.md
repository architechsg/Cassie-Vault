# Mid-Career Enhanced SkillsFuture Credit

## What It Is

The **Mid-Career Enhanced Subsidy (MCES)** and **Mid-Career Enhanced SFC top-up** are separate government schemes targeted at mid-career Singaporeans looking to reskill or upskill. They are **not** the same as the regular SkillsFuture Credit (SFC) balance.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Not all Coursemology courses qualify for Mid-Career Enhanced SFC. The regular SFC credit can be used more broadly. Always confirm eligibility before registering.

## Types of Mid-Career Support Available

### 1. Mid-Career Enhanced Subsidy (MCES) — via SSG
- Available to **Singapore Citizens aged 40 and above**
- Provides an enhanced SSG subsidy of **70%** (same tier as the standard SC 40+ subsidy — already included in [[funding/ssg-subsidies]])
- No separate application needed — applied automatically based on age and citizenship at point of funding claim

### 2. Mid-Career Enhanced SkillsFuture Credit Top-Up — $4,000 credit
- A one-time top-up of **$4,000** given to eligible Singapore Citizens aged 40 and above (specific cohorts — check myskillsfuture.gov.sg for your eligibility)
- This appears as a separate balance in your SFC account alongside your regular credit
- Has its **own approved course list** — not all WSQ courses qualify, even if they have a TGS code
- Cannot be used interchangeably with your regular SFC balance

## How to Check Your Balance

Log in to [myskillsfuture.gov.sg](https://myskillsfuture.gov.sg) with Singpass and click the **arrow** beside your total SFC balance to see the breakdown:

- **Opening / Regular credit** — usable for any eligible WSQ course at any approved provider
- **Mid-Career Enhanced credit** — restricted to the approved course list only

## Does My Course Qualify for Mid-Career Enhanced SFC?

**We cannot confirm this without checking.** The approved course list changes periodically and not all Coursemology courses are on it.

**What to do:**
1. Check your balance breakdown on myskillsfuture.gov.sg first
2. If you have Mid-Career Enhanced credit and want to use it, **contact us before registering** to confirm your specific course qualifies
3. If the claim fails at registration, you will not be able to retroactively change the funding type

> Contact us: **hello@coursemology.sg** or WhatsApp **9866 0772**

Do not assume a course qualifies just because it has a TGS code or is WSQ-accredited.

## Common Mistake to Avoid

If a student selects Mid-Career Enhanced credit during registration for a non-approved course, the SFC claim will fail. This can delay enrolment confirmation. Always verify with us first if you intend to use Mid-Career Enhanced credit.
