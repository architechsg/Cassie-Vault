# SkillsFuture Credit (SFC)

## What It Is
A government scheme giving Singapore Citizens funds to offset course fees. Credits are topped up periodically by the government and can be used at any approved training provider.

## Who Qualifies
Singapore Citizens aged **25 and above**.

## How to Claim (step by step)
1. Find your course on Coursemology.sg and note its **TGS code** (on the course page)
2. Log in to https://myskillsfuture.gov.sg with Singpass
3. Search for the course using the TGS code
4. Submit your SFC claim **before the course start date**
5. Coursemology will be notified — pay any remaining balance when confirmed

> ⚠️ **Critical:** The claim MUST be submitted and approved before the class starts. Claims after the start date are not accepted. Do not leave this to the last minute.

## Which Courses Are Eligible
All WSQ courses with a TGS code are eligible. Private/non-WSQ workshops (no TGS code) are not.

## Can I Stack SFC With SSG Subsidy?
Yes. SSG subsidy reduces the base price first. SFC then offsets the remaining amount you owe after the subsidy.

## Can I Stack SFC With UTAP?
Yes, if you are an NTUC member. See [[funding/utap-ntuc]].
