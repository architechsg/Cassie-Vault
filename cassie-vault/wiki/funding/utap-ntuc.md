# UTAP — NTUC Union Training Assistance Programme

## What It Is
UTAP provides co-funding for NTUC union members who take approved training courses. It subsidises up to 50% of the remaining out-of-pocket course fees (after SSG subsidy).

## Who Qualifies
- NTUC union members
- Must be a member **before** registering for the course

## How Much
- Up to **50% co-funding** on the remaining fees (after SSG subsidy)
- Capped at **$250 per year**

## Which Courses Are Eligible
Courses marked with UTAP = ✓ on their page. Most WSQ courses offered by Coursemology are UTAP-eligible. Check the individual course page to confirm.

## How to Claim
Claim is made through the NTUC U Portal after completing the course. Keep your receipt and attendance records.
