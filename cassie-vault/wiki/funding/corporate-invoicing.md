# Corporate Invoicing — For SME & Company-Sponsored Trainees

This page covers how invoices work when a company is paying for employee training — either for SME-billed courses or corporate bookings.

## Who Issues the Invoice?

Invoices are issued by the Coursemology provider entity responsible for the course:

| Provider | Invoice Abbreviation | Courses Covered |
|---|---|---|
| AesthetiCar Pte Ltd | **ATT** | Food Safety, Maintenance, Baking & Cooking, AI & Tech |
| Associates Consulting Pte Ltd | **AB** | Cleaning, MS Office, Admin/HR, First Aid, Beauty |
| Cantley LifeCare Pte Ltd | **CT** | Drone & Digital Media, Ecommerce, AI & Tech |

## Invoice Number Format

Corporate invoices are numbered in the format: **[Entity]-OLA-[Number]**

Examples:
- `ATT-OLA-12345` — invoice from AesthetiCar Pte Ltd
- `AB-OLA-6789` — invoice from Associates Consulting Pte Ltd
- `CT-OLA-1001` — invoice from Cantley LifeCare Pte Ltd

When referencing an invoice with Coursemology staff, use this full invoice number.

## Payment Terms

Standard payment terms are **Immediate** (payment due upon invoice). For credit terms or any other commercial terms request, contact the corporate training consultant (corporate sales WhatsApp 8899 1945 or hello@coursemology.sg).

## What the Invoice Covers

Each line on the invoice corresponds to one employee's enrolment in a class. The invoice shows:
- Student name and NRIC (partially masked for privacy: `XXXXX` + last 4 digits)
- Course name and reference number
- Class start and end dates
- Course fee (ex-GST), GST amount, and funding breakdown
- Amount payable by the company after government subsidies

## GST

All invoiced amounts are subject to **9% GST** unless the company or student is exempt. GST is computed on the **net course fee after any discount, but before subsidy** — a discount lowers the GST, while a government subsidy lowers the fee payable but not the GST.

## How to Pay

Payment can be made via:
- **Bank transfer** to the issuing entity's bank account (details on the invoice)
- **PayNow** (PayNow details on the invoice)

After payment, please send proof of transfer to **hello@coursemology.sg** or corporate sales WhatsApp **8899 1945** with the invoice number.
