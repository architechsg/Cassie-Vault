# FAQ — General

**Q: What are your operating hours?**
A: Monday to Sunday, 8:30am – 6:00pm. Closed on Public Holidays.

**Q: Where are you located?**
A: Four locations — Toa Payoh (HQ), Jurong East, Tampines, and The Adelphi at City Hall. See [[company/locations]] for full addresses.

**Q: What time do classes start?**
A: Generally 9am–6pm. Food Safety courses run 9am–5:30pm. Exact times are listed on each course page.

**Q: When will I know if my class is confirmed?**
A: You'll receive an SMS and email confirmation after payment is completed.

**Q: Can I pay with NETS or credit card?**
A: No. Accepted methods are Bank Transfer, Cash (Toa Payoh only), SkillsFuture Credit, and PayNow. See [[policies/payment-methods]].

**Q: Can I reschedule or get a refund?**
A: Yes. Submit your request at least 7 calendar days before class for a full refund. Within 7 days, the deposit is forfeited but the course fee is refunded. When rescheduling, your deposit transfers to the new class. See [[policies/refund-cancellation]] and [[policies/reschedule-policy]].

**Q: Is corporate / on-site training available?**
A: Yes. Customised on-site training is available with flexible scheduling, eligible for Absentee Payroll and ETSS subsidies. See [[funding/corporate-funding]] or contact [[company/contact]].

**Q: How do I download my certificate?**
A: WSQ certificates are issued electronically via myskillsfuture.sg. See [[policies/certification]] for steps.

**Q: Can I retake the exam if I fail?**
A: Generally no. Trainees who fail may not be eligible for subsidies and could be charged the full fee. Check with staff for specific courses: [[company/contact]].
