# Reschedule Policy

## Key Rules
- Submit your reschedule request at least **7 calendar days** before the class date
- Your deposit can be **transferred** to the new class (no cash refund required)
- A reschedule admin fee of **$16.35** may apply — this is charged at staff discretion and is rarely enforced in practice
- You must reschedule to the same course, different date

## How to Reschedule
Contact Coursemology before the 7-day cutoff:
- **WhatsApp:** 9866 0772
- **Email:** hello@coursemology.sg

For **corporate/company-sponsored trainees:** your HR or officer-in-charge should liaise directly with the Coursemology sales person who handled the booking.

## Deposit on Reschedule
When you reschedule, your deposit is classified as a **Deposit Transfer** — it moves to your new class. You do not need to pay the deposit again. See [[policies/deposit-policy]].

## Late Reschedule (within 7 days)
Reschedule requests within 7 calendar days of the class are handled case by case. The deposit may be forfeited. Contact staff as early as possible.
