# Refund & Cancellation Policy

## Cancellation by Student

| Timing | Course Fee | Deposit |
|---|---|---|
| More than 7 calendar days before class | Full refund | Returned within 7 days |
| 7 calendar days or fewer before class | Refunded | **Forfeited** |

## Cancellation by Coursemology
If Coursemology cancels or postpones a class:
- 100% full refund (course fee + deposit) within 7 days
- No penalty to the student

## How to Request a Refund
Contact Coursemology before the class date:
- **Email:** hello@coursemology.sg
- **WhatsApp:** 9866 0772

## Prefer Rescheduling?
If you can't attend but want to take the course later, consider rescheduling instead — your deposit can transfer to the new class. See [[policies/reschedule-policy]].

## Deposit Details
See [[policies/deposit-policy]] for deposit amounts and the four refund types used.

## Bank Transfer Refunds
If your refund is to be paid via bank transfer (Finance Bank Refund), you must have your **bank name and account number** registered with Coursemology. Please provide these details when contacting staff to initiate your refund. Without them, the transfer cannot be processed.

## SSG-Funded Enrolments
If you claimed SkillsFuture Credit or SSG subsidy and then cancel, the grant claim may need to be amended separately. Coursemology staff handle this — contact [[company/contact]] for guidance.
