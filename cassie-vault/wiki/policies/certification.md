# Certification & Statement of Attainment

## WSQ Statement of Attainment (SOA)
All WSQ courses award a **Statement of Attainment (SOA)** upon successful completion. The SOA is issued electronically via myskillsfuture.sg.

## How to Download Your Certificate
1. Go to https://www.myskillsfuture.sg
2. Log in with Singpass
3. Download your e-certificate

Step-by-step guide: https://coursemology.sg/how-to-download-statement-of-attainment/

## When Will I Receive It?
You will be notified by **SMS and email** after assessment results are processed. Processing time varies — typically within a few days to a couple of weeks after the course end date.

## Certificate Validity
- **Food Safety Level 1** — valid 5 years; first renewal at 5 years, subsequent renewals every 10 years
- Other WSQ courses — check individual course page for validity period
- Non-WSQ / Private courses — certificate issued by Coursemology, validity stated on the certificate

## Failed Assessment
- Generally **no retakes** for most courses
- Trainees who fail are not eligible for SSG subsidies on that attempt
- You may be charged the full (unsubsidised) course fee
- Confirm retake availability for specific courses with staff: [[company/contact]]
