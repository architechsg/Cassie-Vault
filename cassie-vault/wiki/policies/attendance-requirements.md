# Attendance Requirements

## Minimum Attendance for SSG Funding
To qualify for SSG subsidies (including SkillsFuture Credit), trainees must attend at least **75% of training hours**.

Failing to meet the 75% threshold means:
- SSG funding is not approved
- You may be charged the full (unsubsidised) course fee

## Singpass App — Mandatory for Funded Trainees
All SSG-funded trainees **must use the Singpass App** for electronic attendance. This is a government requirement — without it, attendance cannot be captured and funding will not be approved.

**Download the Singpass App before class day** from the App Store or Google Play.

## Punctuality
- Arrive on time — latecomers may not be allowed into assessments
- Classes generally start at 9:00am (Food Safety courses: 9am–5:30pm; most others: 9am–6pm)

## Assessment
- Must pass all assessments to receive WSQ certification
- Generally **no retakes** — trainees who fail may not be eligible for subsidies and could be charged the full course fee
- Confirm retake policy for specific courses with staff before enrolling

## Who This Applies To
All trainees claiming SSG subsidies, SkillsFuture Credit, or UTAP funding. Private/non-subsidised trainees are not subject to the Singpass attendance requirement.
