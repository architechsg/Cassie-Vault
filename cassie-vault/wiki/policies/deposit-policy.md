# Deposit Policy

## What Is the Deposit?
A refundable **admin deposit** collected from students at enrolment. It is held by Coursemology and returned after the student completes the course.

The deposit is separate from the course fee. It is collected to confirm the student's commitment to attend.

## How Much?
Deposit amounts vary by course — typically **$20–$100**, with higher amounts possible for longer or premium courses (multi-day courses like drone, culinary, or specialist programmes may be higher). The exact deposit for a specific course can be confirmed with staff at time of booking.

## When Is It Refunded?
- **After course completion** — full deposit returned
- **Cancellation more than 7 days before class** — full deposit returned within 7 days
- **Cancellation 7 days or fewer before class** — deposit forfeited
- **Rescheduling** — deposit can transfer to the new class date (no cash refund needed). See [[policies/reschedule-policy]].
- **If Coursemology cancels or postpones** — full deposit + course fee refunded within 7 days

## Refund Types
There are four official refund classifications used internally:
1. **Cash Full Refund** — full deposit returned in cash
2. **Deposit Forfeited** — student cancels late; deposit kept
3. **Finance Bank Refund** — refund sent via bank transfer
4. **Deposit Transfer** — deposit moved to a new class when rescheduling
