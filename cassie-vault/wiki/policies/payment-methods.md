# Payment Methods

## Accepted Methods

| Method | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Bank Transfer** | Main method. Two accounts: OLA Bank Transfer or ATT Bank Transfer depending on course provider. |
| **Cash** | Available at **Toa Payoh location only**. |
| **Cheque** | Accepted. Typically used by corporate clients. |
| **SkillsFuture Credit (SFC)** | Claim at myskillsfuture.gov.sg before course start date using the TGS code. See [[funding/skillsfuture-credit]]. |
| **PayNow** | Available. |
| **PSEA** | Post-Secondary Education Account. Available for eligible courses (check individual course page). |

## Not Accepted
- NETS
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- Stripe / HitPay

> **Note:** Historical records show NETS and card payments were accepted in the past but are no longer active.

## Deposit
A refundable admin deposit is collected at enrolment. See [[policies/deposit-policy]].
